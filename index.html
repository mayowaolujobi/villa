About Us

Set in the heart of Ikeja, Kelsey Greene Villa offers guests an unparalled luxury hotel experience, ample meeting spaces and grandiose accommodation in urban setting.

The Villa is located close to some of the finest tourist attractions and multinational organizations making it the perfect hotel for affluent business and fun-seeking travelers.
Kelsey Greene Villa offers her guests access to fine cosmopolitan cuisine provided by the Villa restaurant and The bush Bar.
